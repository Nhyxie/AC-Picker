AC Villager Picker

Make your perfect village!

Simply select your favorite villager(s) from each batch and hit the "pick" button. If you can't decide you can hit pass. If you make a mistake, hit undo to undo the last selection you made. Eventually batches will get smaller and smaller until you find your favorite! After a favorite is found, the list will be repopulated and you can continue until you find your second favorite, third, fourth, and so on. Not all villagers will be added back into the pool when a favorite is found. If a villager is "defeated" by another (aka you picked a given villager over another), then they won't rejoin the pool until the villager that "defeated" them is added to the favorites list.

This picker was made using the Cave of Dragonflies' Favorite Picker tool which was made to be a customisable, simpler version of their favorite Pokemon picker tool. Theirs is much nicer and has more features than this. Any Pokemon fans can find it at here.
